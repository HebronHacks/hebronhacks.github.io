FAQ

Who can attend?

Hebron Hacks is open to all students. Beginners, intermediate, and advanced coders are all welcome. Anyone interested in coding, design, or technology is encouraged to participate.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is required. This event is designed to be beginner-friendly, and mentors and resources will be available to help you get started.

What kind of projects can I submit?

Projects can be built using any programming language or platform. Projects do not need to be finished to be shared at the end of the event.

Can I work in a team or alone?

You can work individually or in a team of up to four people. Mentors can help you form teams if needed.

Will there be mentors or support?

Yes! Mentors, workshops, and resources will be available throughout the hackathon to help participants.

Is the event free?

Yes! Hebron Hacks is completely free to attend.

Will food be provided?

Pizza and snacks will be available for participants.

Do I need to register in advance?

Yes, please sign up in advance using the registration form. This helps us plan teams, mentors, and materials.

Can I attend virtually?

Unfortunately there is no virtual option at this time

Will there be prizes?

Prizes may be awarded! Details will be announced closer to the event.
